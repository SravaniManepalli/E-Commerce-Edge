About Visualization

What is Visualization?

Visualization transforms complex data into clear, insightful representations. It helps in understanding data by making it accessible and interpretable.

Advantages of Visualization

  • Improves data comprehension
  • Enhances decision-making
  • Identifies trends and patterns
  • Communicates information effectively

When to Use Visualization?

Visualization is useful when dealing with large datasets, identifying trends, comparing data, and presenting complex information in an understandable format.
